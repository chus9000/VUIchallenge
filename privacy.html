Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

When you use VUIchallenge, we collect:

  • Google account information (name, email, profile picture) when you sign in
  • Your challenge progress and completion status
  • Notes you write for challenges
  • Usage analytics through Google Tag Manager

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the VUIchallenge service
  • Save your progress across devices
  • Improve our service through analytics
  • Generate completion certificates

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, analytics, and to remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
